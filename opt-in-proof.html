Luna Messaging Opt-In Process

Luna Messaging uses a public web signup flow for opt-in. Users request access by submitting their mobile number through the signup page and checking an unchecked consent box before any messaging is enabled.

How users opt in

  1. The user visits the Luna Messaging signup page.
  2. The user enters their mobile phone number.
  3. The user checks an unchecked consent box agreeing to receive utility, reminder, and assistant text messages from Luna Messaging.
  4. The signup page includes links to the Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions.
  5. The signup page states that message frequency varies and message and data rates may apply.
  6. After submission, the number is manually reviewed and added to an approved allowlist before messaging is enabled.
  7. Luna sends messages only to approved users in response to the user’s inbound message or for reminder and utility messages specifically requested by the user.
  8. Users can reply HELP for help or STOP to opt out.

Consent checkbox language

By checking this box, I agree to receive utility, reminder, and assistant text messages from Luna Messaging at the mobile number I provided. Message frequency varies based on my interaction and requested reminders. Message and data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help or STOP to opt out. I have read and agree to the Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy.
